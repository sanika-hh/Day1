DCET Info

Essential information to help diploma students prepare with confidence.

What is DCET?

DCET (Diploma CET) is an entrance exam for diploma students to get lateral entry into engineering degree (2nd year) in Karnataka.

Eligibility

  • Diploma students (final year/completed)
  • Minimum marks as per DCET rules
  • Valid documents and verification

Exam Pattern

  • Multiple choice questions (MCQ)
  • Questions from key diploma subjects and fundamentals
  • Time and total marks as per official notification
Refer to the latest official DCET notification for final pattern and updates.

Important Documents

  • SSLC / 10th marks card
  • Diploma marks cards
  • Study certificate / TC (if required)
  • Category certificate (if applicable)
  • Aadhar, photo, signature

How to Apply

  1. Read the official notification carefully.
  2. Fill the application form with correct details.
  3. Upload photo and signature in the required size/format.
  4. Pay the fee (if applicable) and submit.
  5. Download the application and keep it for reference.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Uploading unclear photo or wrong file size
  • Incorrect marks/CGPA entry
  • Missing documents during verification
  • Not checking the final preview before submission
